Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Ezan Vakti (PrayerTime) available on the Google Play Store.

The Ezan Vakti application provides prayer times, Qibla direction, Quran audio playback, Islamic reminders, and religious content sharing features.

Age Restriction

The Ezan Vakti application is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.

This application is not designed for children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Information We Access

The application may request access to certain device permissions in order to provide its core functionality:

  • Location Information: Used to determine the Qibla direction and calculate accurate prayer times. Location data is only used within the app and is not stored on external servers.
  • Notification Permission: Used to send prayer time reminders, important Islamic day notifications, and user-set reminders.
  • Device Information: Anonymous device information may be collected automatically by third-party services such as advertising providers.

The Ezan Vakti application does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, phone number, or user accounts.

Health Data

The Ezan Vakti application does not collect, process, or store any health-related data.

How Information Is Used

The accessed information is used only to provide and improve the following features:

  • Calculating and displaying prayer times
  • Providing Qibla direction
  • Sending prayer time notifications
  • Allowing users to set reminders for important Islamic days
  • Providing Quran audio playback
  • Improving application performance and stability

Data Storage and Retention

User preferences such as notification settings, reminder selections, or application preferences may be stored locally on the user's device using secure local storage.

This information remains stored only while the application is installed on the device.

The Ezan Vakti application does not store personal user data on external servers.

Data Deletion

Users can delete all stored application data at any time by:

  • Clearing the application data from device settings
  • Uninstalling the application from the device

Once the application is removed, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services that may collect anonymous data to improve their services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising Service)

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

Content Sharing

The application allows users to share religious quotes, hadiths, or other Islamic content through external applications such as messaging or social media platforms. Content is shared only when the user explicitly chooses to share it.

Security

We value user privacy and take reasonable steps to protect information processed within the application.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
