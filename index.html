Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the PrayerTime mobile application ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects user information.

By using the application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Age Restriction

The PrayerTime application is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.

This application is not designed for children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Information We Access

The application may access certain device permissions in order to provide its core functionality.

  • Location Data
    The app may access your device location to determine the Qibla direction and calculate accurate prayer times. Location data is used only within the application and is not stored on external servers.
  • Notification Permission
    Notification permission is used to send prayer time reminders, important Islamic day notifications, and reminders created by the user.
  • Device Information
    Anonymous device information may be collected automatically by third-party services such as advertising providers.

The PrayerTime application does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or account information.

Health Data

The PrayerTime application does not collect, process, or store any health-related information.

How Information Is Used

The accessed information is used only to provide and improve the following features:

  • Determining the Qibla direction
  • Calculating and displaying prayer times
  • Sending prayer notifications
  • Setting reminders for important Islamic days
  • Improving application performance

Data Storage and Retention

User preferences such as notification settings or reminder selections may be stored locally on the user's device using secure local storage.

This information remains stored only while the application is installed on the device.

The PrayerTime application does not store personal user data on external servers.

Data Deletion

Users may delete their stored application data at any time by:

  • Clearing the application data from the device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the application.

Once the application is removed, all locally stored information is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services which may collect anonymous information to improve their services.

  • Google AdMob (advertising service)

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

Content Sharing

The application allows users to share religious content such as quotes or hadiths through external applications including messaging or social media platforms. Content is shared only when the user explicitly chooses to share it.

Security

We value user privacy and take reasonable steps to protect information processed within the application.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be published on this page.
