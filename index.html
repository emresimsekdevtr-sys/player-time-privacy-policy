Ezan Vakti Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Ezan Vakti ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information of users who use our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what data the application may access, how it is used, and how users can control their information.

By installing or using the application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Application Overview

Ezan Vakti is an Islamic utility application that provides the following features:

  • Prayer time calculations
  • Qibla direction finder
  • Prayer time notifications
  • Islamic holiday reminders
  • Quran audio listening
  • Sharing Islamic quotes

The application does not require account registration and does not ask users to provide personal identity information.

2. Personal Information

Ezan Vakti does not collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Contacts
  • Photos or videos
  • Messages

No personal user accounts or profiles are created within the application.

3. Health Data

The application does NOT collect, process, store, transmit, or share any Health Data.

Health Data includes information related to a person's physical health, fitness activities, biometric measurements, or medical conditions.

Examples of Health Data that are NOT collected:

  • Heart rate
  • Step count
  • Calories burned
  • Sleep data
  • Exercise or workout information
  • Fitness tracking data
  • Body measurements
  • Medical or biometric records

The application does not access or integrate with health related platforms including:

  • Google Fit
  • Health Connect
  • Apple HealthKit
  • Samsung Health

The application does not request permissions related to activity recognition, body sensors, pedometer data, or any health monitoring features.

4. Device Sensors

The application may access limited device sensors such as the accelerometer and magnetometer (compass).

These sensors are used only to determine the device orientation in order to display the correct Qibla direction.

Sensor data is processed locally on the user's device and is not stored, transmitted, recorded, or shared.

Sensor data is NOT used for health monitoring, fitness tracking, activity tracking, or biometric measurements.

5. Location Data

The application may request access to the device's location.

Location data is used only for:

  • Calculating accurate prayer times based on geographic location
  • Determining the correct Qibla direction

Location information is processed locally on the device and is not stored on external servers.

6. Notifications

The application may request permission to send notifications.

Notifications are used only for:

  • Prayer time reminders
  • Ezan notifications
  • Islamic holiday reminders

Notifications are not used for marketing or promotional purposes.

7. Calendar Access

The application may request calendar permission to allow users to add reminders for Islamic religious days.

The application does not read existing calendar events and does not collect calendar data.

8. Local Storage

The application may store limited data locally on the user's device including:

  • Prayer time calculation preferences
  • Notification settings
  • User reminder settings
  • Application configuration

This information remains only on the user's device and is not transmitted to external servers.

9. Advertising

The application may display advertisements using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

Advertising providers may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Approximate location
  • App interaction information

This information is collected directly by the advertising provider and processed according to their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

10. Automatically Collected Technical Information

Some technical information may be automatically collected in order to improve application stability and performance.

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash logs

This information is used only for debugging and improving the application.

11. Data Retention

The application does not store personal data on external servers.

Local settings remain on the device until the user clears the application data or uninstalls the application.

12. Children's Privacy

The application is not directed specifically to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
